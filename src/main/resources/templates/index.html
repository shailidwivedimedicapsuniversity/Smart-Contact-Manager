My Dearest Bhupendra

I hope this letter finds you in the best of health and spirits. I don't know if you remember me, but I am the girl who sat behind you in sphinx solution test. I have admired you from afar for so long, and I can no longer keep my feelings to myself.

I hope that you are happy and that you find the love and joy that you deserve. And if our paths do cross again, I hope that you will remember me.

With all my love, Shaili
